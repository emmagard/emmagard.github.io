ABOUT EMMA

Me and my pup!

Hello, world! My name is Emma. I live in Boston, just off of the red line. I grew up just south of the city in a small town called Cohasset. I graduated from Simmons College in 2006 (see some of my drawings in the photo carousel below), worked a short stint in retail, and then drove cross-country to Los Angeles, CA. While there, I worked in a privately held commercial real estate company. After 4 years of sunshine I drove back across the country to good old New England. I dabbled in sewing and fashion design and found myself back in retail. Growing bored with sales, I began exploring other hands on activities outside of work, like gardening and coding! Since giving coding a shot, I've found that I really like it. It's fun, hands on, creative, and certainly a challenge.
